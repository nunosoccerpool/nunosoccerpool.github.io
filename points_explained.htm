GROUP STAGE
3 pts - Correctly predict the 1st place team in a group.
4 pts - Correctly predict the 1st place team in a group + any other rank (1st and last, 1st and 2nd or 1st and 3rd)
7 pts - Correctly predict the entire group's order (1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 4th place)

KNOCKOUT STAGE
Round-of-16
3 pts - For each team correctly predicted to reach the Round-of-16.
2 pts - For correctly predicting two teams would face each other in Round-of-16
2 pts - Correctly predict the exact score of a Round-of-16 match  ("exact score" refers to the score before penalty shootout)
Quarter-Finals
12 pts - For each team correctly predicted to reach the Quarter-Finals.
4 pts - For correctly predicting two teams would face each other in the Quarter-Finals.
4 pts - Correctly predict exact score of a Quarter-Final match.  (must have predicted that both teams would face each other)
Semi-Finals
24 pts - For each team correctly predicted to reach the Semi-Finals.
8 pts - For correctly predicting two teams would face each other in the Semi-Finals.
8 pts - Correctly predict exact score of a Semi-Final match.  (must have predicted that both teams would face each other)
Finals
50 pts - For each team correctly predicted to reach the Finals.
25 pts - Correctly predict exact score of the Finals match.  (must have predicted that both teams would face each other)
75 pts - Correctly predict the Euro 2024 Champion
